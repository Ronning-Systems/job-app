# Job Description: Instrumentation and Control Systems Engineer — BWRX Small Modular Reactor

## Job Title
Instrumentation and Control Systems for the BWRX Small Modular Reactor

## Job Location
Fully remote, limited travel

## Start Date
ASAP

## Working Model
Fully remote, limited travel

## Travel
Potential vendor or customer site visits; GEV office. Customer will be in Canada. Candidates should be able to travel to Canada and the US.

## Working Hours
No preference on time zone. 9 am – 3 pm EST core working hours.

## Interview Process
Virtual, with cameras on, 1 round.

## Must Have
- Instrumentation and controls
- Nuclear
- System engineering

## Position Summary
The System Engineer designs and manages complex Instrumentation and Control systems for the BWRX Small Modular Reactor to ensure optimal performance and reliability.

## Responsibilities
- Develop sub-system requirements from higher level plant requirements.
- Implement system designs based on project requirements.
- Monitor system design progress and address technical issues.
- Document system activities and prepare detailed reports.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to optimize system functionality.
- Ensure compliance with engineering standards and best practices.
- Reactor Protector System and Neutron Monitoring System.

## Qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Engineering or related field.
- Experience in system engineering or technical roles.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Knowledge of system tools and methodologies.
